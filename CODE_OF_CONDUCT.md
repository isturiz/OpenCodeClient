# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

Participants should be constructive, assume good intent, respect differing viewpoints, and focus
criticism on ideas and code rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, publication of
private information, and sustained disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation
when behavior violates these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. All
reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project publicly.
